KOALAS

Native to Australia, koalas are marsupials known for their fluffy ears and calm demeanor. They feed primarily on eucalyptus leaves and spend most of their time sleeping or resting...

Visit the koala enclosure to learn about their habitat, unique digestive system, and the challenges they face due to deforestation and climate change...

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the koalas at pen K05, beside the eucalyptus trail.
